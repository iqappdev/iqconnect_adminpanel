About the App

IQ Connect Mobile App brings Larix Broadcast’s bonded transmission technology to mobile phones and tablets, combining Wi-Fi and cellular networks to reach optimal video quality.

Cover breaking news and events as they happen simply by using your smartphone or tablet to Transmit live video back to the studio.

Requires Android version 4.1 and up.

-We have introduced a powerful management interface for your video files and their associated metadata. This enables some exciting new workflows such as the ability to do Live & Store and then upload that file to YouTube right from the device.

-Are you in an area with zero connectivity but plan to upload your video later? We've now added a "Store" mode that allows you to use IQ Connect to record your video, manage it in the file manager. We feel this will only deepen your love of Smart as we now offer modes to Go Live, Live & Store, and Store Only!

-Getting annoyed when AI takes totally control of your option? Stuck in middle of auto settings? We have cleared a path for these problems, where our users can exit the auto selection in our application and enter their location and their preferred details manually. Even you can select the location you are far apart and set it as a current location.

-Not sure what channels are available? Well we have provided a neat and clear UI to make you understand the channel that is currently available to fetch your live. The list of channels will be displayed and you can find a red tag stating whether the channel is on live or not.

What's new in IQ Connect?

- Here at IQ Connect we know the need to be live now sometimes means you'll be live under non- optimal streaming conditions. To this end we've added the ability to record a full resolution copy of your live stream... while live!

-Video without metadata is like bread without butter. We've added a new metadata feature to be able to send both users entered and automatically generated information on session start.

-Need a lower delay stream? How about a higher bitrate one? Great news! IQ Connect now supports delays as low as 5 seconds, and bitrates up to 3 Mbps! Tread lightly on the higher bitrate, if you're on a capped data plan.

-Location service now supports three modes of operation: 1- never send your location, 2- send your location only while IQ Connect is running, and 3- Always send your location. If you're looking to use IQ Connect's "Be First," make sure you've got location services set to Always
